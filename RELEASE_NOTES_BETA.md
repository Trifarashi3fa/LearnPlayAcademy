# LearnPlay Academy Closed Beta Release Notes

## Release Type

Controlled closed beta candidate for parent and child testing.

## Included Features

- LearnPlay Academy landing and subject navigation.
- Parent registration, login, logout, and account area.
- One-child MVP profile setup with nickname, Year 1, and avatar choice.
- Mathematics Year 1 Forest World active gameplay.
- 10 Mathematics Forest levels with 100 approved questions.
- Question flow with visual learning, explanations, LearnBot guidance, XP, stars, and rewards.
- Forest World map with progression locking.
- Level completion and Forest completion reward flow.
- Parent Dashboard with MVP-safe progress summaries and recommended next learning.
- Safe local progress reset confirmation.
- Vercel Analytics and Speed Insights integration.

## Subjects Available

| Subject | Beta Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mathematics Year 1 | Active | Forest World is the main beta learning experience. |
| English Year 1 | Staging-ready content only | Level 1 approved; gameplay not active in this package. |
| Science | Coming Soon | No gameplay content active. |

| Life Skills / AI Literacy | Coming Soon | Public routes may be gated/unavailable. |

## Limitations

- English gameplay is not active yet.
- English Levels 2-10 remain inactive and in Review.
- No premium payment or subscription flow is active.
- No assessment engine or PDF report is active.
- Parent Dashboard does not claim true weak-topic analytics yet.
- Closed beta should use tester guidance and should not be treated as final public launch.

4. Continue quality optimization for English Levels 2-10.
